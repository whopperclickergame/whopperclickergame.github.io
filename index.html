The realm of soft buns, juicy patties and crunchy lettuce is calling for an experienced cook! You have a chance to generate as many burgers as you please by accomplishing one simple action, so don’t stop and prepare as many as you can! Whopper Clicker has plenty of opportunities layered up for you, so try not to miss any.

Arrange a Perfect Whooper

In Whopper Clicker your major task is pretty simple – to construct as many delicious patties as possible. However, the game requires a careful planning of your finances, as each upgrade costs money. Decide how you want to enhance your creation this time and increase its price with each improvement.

  • Multiply your sandwich
  • Enhance its taste
  • Add new ingredients
  • Acquire surprising rewards

Although you will need to spend more cash on more valuable improvements, their price doesn’t grow. Thus, you may purchase multiple items of the same kind, increasing the production in proportion to the budget you invest.

Create a Yummy Filling

The taste of a sandwich is formed by the filling you put in it and the game has a lot of options to offer you. Whopper Clicker offers you to start with a classic combo – two buns and some meat in between, proceeding to more interesting combinations. The unblocked version of the game, though, gives you access to all the ingredients at once, facilitating your coolinary journey.

Accomplish Interesting Tasks

Not only can you develop your manufacture in Whopper Clicker, but you can also receive numerous rewards for your actions! Each milestone in the game is celebrated by unique achievements that indicate your success.
